How to build a good Overworld Wither Defence (Layered) by Rzghuul

To build an atleast decent wither defence you should follow these rules

Rule 1

No symmetry in terms of groups of 3 Blocks. (more info coming soon or in rzghuul's discord)

Rule 2

The the centere blocks of the witherbore should be forced to hit lava.

Rule 3

There should atleast an equal amount of lavasorces and watersorces. Better would be more lavasources.

Rule 4

No 1x1 Lava sources

Rule 5

If you have Flowing water have extra protection against fast bores

Rule 6

Have lava sources that don't have any water diagonal to them (this only works if water does not touch the floor).

Rule 7

have at least one lava scorce in the path of the witherbore that does not have water infront of it.

Rule 8

Test your wither defences!
